About Me

I’m a Junior Architect at DDIR Architecture Studio working with Dominic Dube. I graduated from RV College of Architecture with a Bachelors in Architecture. Architecture continually motivates me to strive for excellence, embrace innovation, and wholeheartedly appreciate intricate details. I am particularly captivated by Sustainable Architecture and the exploration of novel concepts, which constantly drive me to expand my knowledge, adapt, and continually improve.
